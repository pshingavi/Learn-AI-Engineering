## Usage Notes

1. **For Headers**: Use Prompts 1, 14, 17, 18 for chapter headers and finale
2. **For Concepts**: Use Prompts 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 inline with explanations
3. **For Analogies**: Use Prompts 7, 8, 9 next to their respective sections
4. **For Processes**: Use Prompts 10, 11, 15 for technical explanations
5. **For Characters**: Use Prompt 12 for story elements
6. **For Diagrams**: Use Prompt 16 icons in flowcharts
7. **For Finale**: Use Prompts 13, 17, 18 for the conclusion

**Recommended Aspect Ratios:**
- Header images: 16:9 (landscape)
- Concept illustrations: 4:3 or 1:1 (square)
- Journey maps: 3:2 or 16:9 (landscape)
- Character designs: 3:4 (portrait)
- Icons: 1:1 (square)
- Finale/badge: 1:1 (square)

**Style Consistency Tips:**
- This chapter uses celestial colors (deep blues, golds, silver, starlight)
- Maintain the fantasy aesthetic from previous chapters
- Navigator Nyx should feel cosmic and wise
- Technical diagrams should still have magical touches
- The finale should bring together visual elements from ALL chapters
- Keep the apprentice character consistent throughout

**Color Palette Summary Across All Chapters:**
- Chapter 1 (Vibe Check): Purples, golds, warm browns
- Chapter 2 (RAG): Library browns, glowing blues, parchment yellows
- Chapter 3 (Agents): Copper, brass, steampunk teals
- Chapter 4 (Graphs): Deep blues, celestial golds, starlight silver
- Combined: All four palettes should blend in finale images
