## Usage Notes

1. **For Headers**: Use Prompts 1, 13 for chapter headers and transitions
2. **For Concepts**: Use Prompts 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 inline with explanations
3. **For Analogies**: Use Prompts 7, 8, 9 next to their respective sections
4. **For Processes**: Use Prompts 10, 11, 14, 15 for technical explanations
5. **For Characters**: Use Prompt 12 for story elements
6. **For Diagrams**: Use Prompt 16 icons in flowcharts

**Recommended Aspect Ratios:**
- Header images: 16:9 (landscape)
- Concept illustrations: 4:3 or 1:1 (square)
- Process diagrams: 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1
- Icons: 1:1 (square)

**Style Consistency Tips:**
- Maintain the warm library colors (browns, golds, soft purples)
- Keep the fantasy/magical aesthetic from Chapter 1
- Books should always have a slight glow
- Characters should be consistent with Chapter 1 designs
- Educational diagrams can be more modern but include magical accents
