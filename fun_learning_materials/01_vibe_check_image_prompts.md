## Usage Notes

1. **For Headers**: Use Prompts 1, 9 for chapter headers
2. **For Concepts**: Use Prompts 2, 3, 4, 5, 11 inline with explanations
3. **For Analogies**: Use Prompts 6, 7, 8, 10 next to their respective sections
4. **For Characters**: Use Prompts 13, 14 for story elements throughout
5. **For End of Chapter**: Use Prompt 12 for the oath section

**Recommended Aspect Ratios:**
- Header images: 16:9 (landscape)
- Concept illustrations: 4:3 or 1:1 (square)
- Character sheets: 3:4 (portrait)
- Infographics: 1:1 or 4:5

**Style Consistency Tips:**
- Keep color palette consistent (purples, golds, warm browns)
- Maintain the "friendly fantasy" aesthetic throughout
- Characters should have simple, expressive features
- Avoid overly realistic styles - keep it storybook-like
